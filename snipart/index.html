Born and raised in the thriving suburbia of Purmerend, Robbin obtained his B.Sc. in Biology from the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam in 2013 and soon tossed out the microscope to pursue his passion for tattoo art. With an eye for detail and love for all-things Halo/hip-hop Robbin currently inks at Antiek Tattoo in Amsterdam.
