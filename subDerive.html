Background

Dérive is an interactive virtual guestbook created for travelers; those waiting in the interstitial spaces such as airports or hotels.

With Leap Motion Technologies, Travellers can sign their names in air, indicate their origins and destinations, then a unique star will be generated based on features of their signatures to be placed alongside other travelers' stars in the virtual universe.

Users can then explore the universe to see other people's comments as they pop up on each star. The user can then choose to contribute their own tips or feelings by scanning the QR code and inputting on their smartphones. A globe gesture will transition the universe view to a globe view where travelers can see where their fellow travelers came from and where they are going to.
