UX & Design

Perspective

As a designer, I strive to create exciting solutions and uncover the unexpected. I am passionate about how we experience and incorporate design within our daily lives and question what is necessary for interactions to be intuitive; paying close attention to detail, narrative, and visual procession; in this way I start with the complex and whittle down to the simplest expression of a project's essence- essentially creating functional forms with magic.

Background

I have ~8 years of experience designing for a broad range of cross-disciplinary projects, incorporating UX design methodology across media, and vice versa. I earned my MS in Computer Science with a focus in Human Computer Interaction at Stanford University in 2015 and my BA in Art Practice in 2012.
