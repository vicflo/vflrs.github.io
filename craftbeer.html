Background

Mikkeller was founded by Mikkel Borg Bjergsø, a high school teacher, and journalist Kristian Klarup Keller (a childhood friend. They began as homebrewers, experimenting and cloning other edgy beers of the time they respected, eventually founding the company in 2006.
